About

TThe Columbia China Prospects Conference (CCPC), launched in 2010, has evolved into one of the most influential student-organized conferences on the topic of China. Both Chinese and American leaders join CCPC to inspire the young generation to make a positive impact on the world. Previous CCPC speakers include Gordon Bajnai, former Prime Minister of Hungary; Thomas Fingar, former Principal Assistant Secretary of the United States; Jeffrey Sachs, Director of the UN Millennium Project; Xiaonian Xu, renowned Chinese Economist; Jeffrey Lehman, former President of Cornell University; Shi Wang, Chairman of the Board of Directors of China Vanke Co., Ltd.; Yongchun Lang, famous CCTV host and et al. CCPC was also featured in major Chinese media, both in China and United States: CCTV, Xinhua, China Daily, China Youth Daily, Phoenix, Sina and so on. Therefore, the sparkle of CCPC has been widespread and influenced China’s structure of development strategies. For more information about the previous Columbia China Prospects Conferences, please visit: CCPC 2013, CCPC 2012, CCPC 2011.

哥伦比亚大学中国展望论坛创立于 2010 年。论坛现已发展为美东地区规模最大,最具影响力的中国论坛之一。论 坛聚焦中国当前发展状况,着眼中国未来发展前景,探索中国未来发展道路,造就了一个各领域领军人物汇聚于斯、碰撞思 维、各展雄长的平台。匈牙利前国家首相 Gordon Bajnai、美国前首席助理国务卿 Thomas Fingar、联合国千年计划总负责 人 Jeffrey Sachs、中国著名经济学家许小年、康奈尔大学第十一任校长 Jeffrey Lehman、万科地产董事长王石、中央电视台 《新闻联播》主持人郎永淳、美丽中国创始人潘勋卓等各界嘉宾都曾在此点燃了思想的火花。每届论坛都吸引了近千名来自 美国知名高校的莘莘学子以及社会各界人士参加,并受到央视、新华社、中国日报、中国青年报、凤凰网、新浪与光明网等 多家中美主流媒体的密切关注。因此,与会嘉宾在此平台上发出的声音得到了广泛传播,对中国未来的发展思路产生了潜移 默化的影响。 欲了解更多论坛信息,请参看往届哥伦比亚大学中国展望论坛官方网页 : CCPC 2013, CCPC 2012, CCPC 2011.

Team

主办方介绍

Founded in 1996, Columbia University Chinese Students and Scholars Association (CUCSSA) is one the most prominent non-profit Chinese organizations in the United States. Since its very first day, CUCSSA has been following its mission of promoting cultural and intellectual exchanges between China and the United States. Thanks to the support of Columbia University and its partners, CUCSSA becomes a major platform where Chinese and American students exchange ideas in economy, culture, business, education and many other topics.

哥伦比亚大学中国学生学者联谊会成立于 1996 年, 是全美最具影响力和知名度的华人组织之一。自创立以来, 哥大学 联一直致力于促进中美两国文化和思想的交流。依托哥伦比亚大学的平台并借助战略合作伙伴的支持,哥大学联已经将自身 打造为两国青年在经济、文化、商业与教育等诸多领域开展交流活动的重要平台.
